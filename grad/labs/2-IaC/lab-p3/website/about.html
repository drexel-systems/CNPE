About NovaSpark

Building the future of cloud engineering, one deployment at a time.

🌱 Our Story

NovaSpark was founded in 2024 by a team of platform engineers who were tired of watching great software get stuck in slow, brittle deployment pipelines. We'd all been at companies where "just deploy it" meant a 2-hour Confluence doc, a Slack message to three different teams, and a prayer to the oncall gods.

We decided there had to be a better way. Our whole platform is defined in code, reviewed in pull requests, and deployed in minutes. We practice what we preach — every resource in our stack is version-controlled, reproducible, and destroyable with a single command.

🏗️ How We Build

We're an AWS-first shop that takes Infrastructure as Code seriously. Every EC2 instance, S3 bucket, and IAM role is defined in Pulumi — not clicked into existence in the AWS Console and forgotten about.

Security is baked in from the start: our EC2 instances authenticate to AWS services using IAM roles with temporary, auto-rotating credentials. There are zero hard-coded access keys in our codebase. Zero. If you find one, you get a free lunch.
